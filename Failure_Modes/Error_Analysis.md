#### Common Error analysis reveals three common failure types in the GITes model-generated responses

1) Lack of Actionable Guidance, where the model offers spiritual reassurance but no practical steps
2) Superficial Scripture Reframing, where stress is framed positively without addressing emotional struggle
3) Incomplete Scripture Grounding, where spiritual concepts are mentioned but not clearly tied to the core teaching of the scripture
